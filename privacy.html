Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 5, 2026

EstateConvert ("the app") helps users convert property area units and currencies. This policy explains how we handle information when you use the app.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or sell personal information.

The app does not require account registration and does not ask for your name, phone number, email, address, or payment information.

2. Currency Rates & Network Requests

To provide currency conversion, the app may fetch exchange-rate data from a public data provider over the internet. These requests are used only to retrieve rate information.

  • We do not include personally identifiable data in these requests.
  • Your device may send standard technical information as part of internet communication (e.g., IP address) to the provider, which is typical for any web request.

3. Advertising / Analytics (If Enabled)

If the app includes ads (e.g., Google AdMob) or analytics in the future, those third-party services may collect information such as device identifiers, approximate location, and interaction events for ad delivery and measurement. If/when enabled, we will update this policy and the App Store privacy disclosures accordingly.

4. Data Storage

Area conversions are performed locally. The app may store app preferences on your device (such as last-selected units) using iOS local storage. This data stays on your device.

5. Children’s Privacy

The app is not intended to collect personal information from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised date.
